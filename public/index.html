Javascript

Extensive experience on the use of Jquery as utility library and lately complementing usually complemented with Underscorejs.

Generally I create interactions optimized for smoothness and low power consumption, relying as much as I can in CSS transitions and animations, and simple maintainable JS code.

I don't consider Javascript a toy language and I'm pretty aware of the bad habits usually linked to this language. I keep Cockford's Javascript: The Good Parts close to my bedhead and currently I'm getting deepinto learning good Javascript Patterns.

I have been using in production environments AngularJS and Knockoutjs in order to give the usersresponsive web applications.I also have used Backbone during the exploratory weeks prior to the selection of AngularJS as mainfrontend framework.Plenty of experience using third party APIs, Plugins and Widgets.

HTML & CSS

Really fast at writing clean, efficient, and semantically correct HTML & CSS.

I always apply graceful degradation philosophy to my works, due to my extensive experience I almost unconsciously apply styling patterns that result in posterior little or no crossbrowser debugging.

I have a good understanding of the pros and cons of SMACSS and use it in production environments.

I have experience with both SCSS and LESS and I'm pretty aware of the best practices to make them a clean and efficient compiled CSS.
